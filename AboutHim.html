His Personal Life

Gene Wolfe was born on May 7th, 1931 in New York City. He was diagnosed with polio as a young child. At age 6, he and his family moved to Houston. He attended Lamar High School in Houston and Texas A&M University. During his Junior year in college, he dropped out to fight in the Korean War. After returning from the war, he earned his degree from the University of Houston and became an Industrial Engineer. He lived in Illinois where he raised his four children. Wolfe died in his home on April 14th, 2019.
